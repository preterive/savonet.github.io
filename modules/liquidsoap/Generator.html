Module Generator

module Generator: sig .. end

Operations on generators, which are like FIFO for multimedia data. They are efficiently handling small chunks of data (as found in frames) without copy, and also metadata.

exception Incorrect_stream_type

Raised when trying to feed a generator with data of incorrect type (wrong number of audio channels, etc.).
